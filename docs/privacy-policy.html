Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

✓  Ramadan Times has no servers, collects no analytics, shows no ads, and requires no account. All your data stays on your device. The only data that briefly leaves your device is your location when the app resolves a city name via your device's built-in geocoding service (Google on Android, Apple on iOS).

1. Overview

Ramadan Times ("the app", "we", "us") is a free, offline-first prayer times application for Android developed by Asif Chowdhury. This privacy policy explains what information is used by the app and how it is handled.

We do not operate any servers, databases, or back-end infrastructure. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information on our end.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us or any third party under our control:

  • Location (last known coordinates & city name) — cached in device storage to avoid repeated GPS look-ups. Used solely to calculate accurate prayer times via the offline adhan library.
  • Prayer completion logs — daily records of which prayers you marked as completed and optional timer durations, stored in a local Hive database.
  • App settings & preferences — calculation method, Madhab, time format, notification preferences — stored in SharedPreferences on your device.

All locally stored data is removed when you uninstall the app.

3. Permissions and Why They Are Required

Permission Purpose Data scope
ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION
ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION		 Obtain your GPS coordinates to calculate accurate prayer times for your location. You may also enter a city manually to avoid granting this permission. On-device
INTERNET Used only when resolving a city name from your coordinates (reverse geocoding) or when you search for a city by name. Requests go to your device's platform geocoding service — not to our servers. Platform geocoding
POST_NOTIFICATIONS To deliver Sehri reminders, Iftar alerts, and per-prayer notifications at the times you configure. On-device
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM To schedule notifications at precise prayer times, even when the device is in a low-power state. Requires a one-time grant via Android Settings → Alarms & Reminders. On-device
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED To reschedule prayer notifications after the device restarts. On-device
HIGH_SAMPLING_RATE_SENSORS To read the device's compass sensor at sufficient frequency for the Qibla direction feature. On-device

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or crash-reporting services. The only third-party interaction is:

  • Platform Geocoding (Android: Google Geocoding API; iOS: Apple CoreLocation / Maps) — When you search for a city by name, or when the app resolves your GPS coordinates to a readable city name, the query is handled by your device's built-in geocoding service. On Android this is provided by Google; on iOS by Apple. The data sent (coordinates or a city name string) is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and Apple's Privacy Policy respectively. We do not receive or store this data.
  • Google Fonts (Poppins typeface) — The app bundles the Poppins font locally at build time. No fonts are downloaded at runtime; no requests are made to Google Fonts servers.

5. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not collect, store, or sell any personal data.
  • We do not display advertisements.
  • We do not require account creation or login.
  • We do not use analytics or crash-reporting SDKs (e.g. Firebase, Sentry).
  • We do not share any data with data brokers or marketing companies.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.

6. Data Retention

All data (cached location, prayer logs, settings) is stored exclusively on your device. It is retained until you clear the app's data or uninstall the app. Because we hold no data on our servers, there is nothing to delete on our end.

7. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. No registration is required to use any feature of the app.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
